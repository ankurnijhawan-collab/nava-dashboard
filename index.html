Welcome to NAVA
Your AI clinical intelligence assistant — built for Indian doctors.

Today's OPD — your home screen
See your patient queue, urgent flags, and quick actions. Pick a specialty from the left panel.
→
Open a patient to see their full case
Tap any patient card to open their history, vitals, labs, and medications in one view.
+
Add a new patient in seconds
Click "New Patient", enter the basics, then run NAVA analysis. No paperwork needed.
N
Run Analysis — NAVA's core feature
Open a patient, then click "Run Analysis" at the bottom left. NAVA will rank likely diagnoses, flag what not to miss, and suggest treatment.
◉
Record your consultation
Use "Record Consult" to speak in Hindi or English. NAVA auto-generates structured notes — no typing needed.
℞
Generate prescriptions instantly
After analysis, click "Generate Rx". NAVA pre-fills the prescription based on the diagnosis — you just review and print.
✓
NAVA works without an API key
Demo mode uses built-in cases so you can explore every feature right now. No account needed.
⚡
Add a Claude API key for real AI
In the NAVA Analysis panel, tap "+ Claude AI" and paste your Anthropic API key. NAVA will then analyse your real patients with live AI — not demo data.
i
Your data stays on your device
Nothing is stored on a server. Keys and notes stay in your browser only.
